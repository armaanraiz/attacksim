About AttackSim

A comprehensive security awareness training platform designed to protect organizations through education and simulation.

Our Mission

AttackSim empowers individuals and organizations to build robust cybersecurity defenses through hands-on training and realistic threat simulations.

We believe that the best defense against cyber threats is education, awareness, and practice in a safe, controlled environment.

Safe Learning Environment

All simulations are conducted ethically with full consent

Realistic Training

Experience real-world attack scenarios without real-world consequences. Learn from mistakes in a safe environment.

Our Educational Approach

Learn by Doing

AttackSim is committed to ethical cybersecurity education. We believe in building defenses, not exploiting vulnerabilities.

  • No real data collection or exploitation
  • Compliance with privacy laws and regulations
